Clinical Services

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Specialist-led care covering all aspects of women's reproductive health — from conception to delivery and beyond.

🚨
Emergency Contact: 0713 309 093

For obstetric emergencies, sudden heavy bleeding, severe abdominal pain or any urgent concern, call the emergency line immediately or proceed to the nearest emergency department.

🤱

Antenatal Care

Comprehensive pregnancy monitoring

  • Booking visit and initial pregnancy assessment
  • Serial ultrasound scans (dating, anomaly, growth)
  • Full blood count and blood group testing
  • Glucose tolerance testing (GDM screening)
  • Blood pressure monitoring and pre-eclampsia screening
  • Foetal wellbeing assessment and biophysical profile
  • Anaemia management and iron supplementation guidance
  • Birth planning and delivery preparation counselling
👶

Labour & Delivery

Normal and complicated deliveries

  • Normal vaginal delivery with specialist oversight
  • Elective and emergency caesarean section
  • Assisted delivery (vacuum and forceps)
  • Management of breech presentations
  • Management of prolonged or obstructed labour
  • Post-partum haemorrhage prevention and management
  • Perineal repair and post-delivery care
  • Neonatal resuscitation support
🔬

General Gynaecology

Investigative and medical management

  • Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) diagnosis and management
  • Uterine fibroid assessment and management
  • Endometriosis evaluation
  • Abnormal uterine bleeding investigation
  • Cervical screening (Pap smear)
  • Ovarian cyst assessment
  • Pelvic pain evaluation
  • Vulval and vaginal conditions
📡

Ultrasound Imaging

In-room diagnostic imaging

  • Early pregnancy (viability) scan
  • First trimester nuchal translucency scan
  • Second trimester anomaly scan (20-week scan)
  • Growth and wellbeing scans (third trimester)
  • Pelvic ultrasound for gynaecological conditions
  • Transvaginal ultrasound
  • Doppler studies (umbilical and uterine artery)
💊

Contraception

Personalised family planning

  • Combined oral contraceptive counselling and prescription
  • Progestogen-only pill
  • Injectable contraceptives (Depo-Provera)
  • Subdermal implant insertion and removal
  • Intrauterine device (IUD / coil) insertion and removal
  • Emergency contraception
  • Post-partum contraception planning
🩺

Fertility Assessment

Initial subfertility investigation

  • Ovulation confirmation and cycle monitoring
  • Hormonal profile (FSH, LH, AMH, thyroid)
  • Pelvic ultrasound for follicle tracking
  • Hysterosalpingography (tubal patency) assessment
  • Semen analysis coordination
  • Referral to assisted reproduction when indicated

Specialist Women's Health Care in Harare

Dr. S.W. Madombi is a fully qualified Obstetrician and Gynaecologist holding both a primary medical degree (MBChB) and a postgraduate Masters in Medicine in Obstetrics and Gynaecology, both obtained from the University of Zimbabwe.

Practising from Central Medical Rooms at 68 Central Avenue, Harare, Dr. Madombi provides comprehensive specialist care covering the full spectrum of women's reproductive health — from pre-conception counselling and antenatal care through to delivery, post-natal care, and the management of complex gynaecological conditions.

The practice is equipped with an in-room ultrasound machine, enabling diagnostic imaging at the point of care. This allows timely assessment of both obstetric and gynaecological conditions without the need for external referral for most routine imaging.

Our Approach

Every patient is seen as an individual. Consultations are unhurried, questions are encouraged, and clinical decisions are explained clearly. The aim is to ensure patients leave with a sound understanding of their condition and the reasoning behind any management plan.

For obstetric patients, continuity of care is emphasised — ideally the same specialist throughout the pregnancy journey. For gynaecological patients, the goal is accurate diagnosis and evidence-based management, with referral to appropriate sub-specialists when the clinical picture demands it.
